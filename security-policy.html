Security policy

Monkadex by Simonkamon11

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to:

aarsethsimon0@gmail.com


Please do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had an opportunity to investigate and address it.


Please include: - A description of the vulnerability - Steps to reproduce the issue - Affected components or URLs - Any proof-of-concept details - The potential impact


We are forever greatful for your help. Thank you.
